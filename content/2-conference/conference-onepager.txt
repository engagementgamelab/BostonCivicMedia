Title: Boston Civic Media Conference 2015

----

Text: 

The Boston Civic Media Conference will bring together researchers and practitioners in the Boston area to collaboratively explore the methodologies and metrics appropriate for the study of civic technology. The day will start with a private, invitation only conference, followed by a public panel and cocktail reception. The meeting and public event will focus on key questions that explore how best research and practice can support a more sustainable infrastructure for civic technology in Boston. 

The event will gather researchers from a range of fields including sociology, policy, communications, and education, to discuss the affordances and limits of measurement in the applied field of civic technology. It will also gather practitioners from government and NGOs in the Boston area to talk about the challenges of research in practice.
